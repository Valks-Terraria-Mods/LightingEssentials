A lighting overhaul mod for terraria.

Surface plants glow a dim green.
Moss glow based on their colors.
The jungle is lit green all over slightly.
Life crystals, life fruit and hearts glow red.
Cactus glows a dim orange.
Gems glow based on their colors.
Prehardmode ores glow a dim white while hardmode ores glow more vibrant colors.
Meteorite and hellstone are vibrant red.
Containers and debris glow a dim white.
